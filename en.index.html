ABOUT US

We are a leader in the field of logistics services.

Our company was founded in 2015 as a family business with 15 years of professional experience. Today, the number of our team has exceeded 20 people. In logistics, we serve our customers with special refrigerated, boxed, double-decker, tail lift trailers and modern Euro6 tractors. In Szigetszentmiklós, we supply our clients' needs in our 600m2 covered, ramped warehouse, and our collection goods are handled here.
